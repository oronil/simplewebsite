ABOUT US

BLIX is a trustless, and non-custodial financial L2 protocol, governed entirely by Blix token holders via a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO).

With a mission to transfer traditional financial instruments into the DeFi space, we are committed to simplify the DeFi space and provide tools every investor need to find the right protocol to put his assets to work.
